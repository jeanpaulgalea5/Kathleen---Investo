BUY section legend

Entry Zone is relative to each ticker's own historical RSI at past strategy entry points, not a fixed universal RSI rule.
  • Lower Quartile – current daily RSI is in the lowest 25% of this ticker's historical entry RSI readings.
  • Normal Range – current daily RSI is between the ticker's 25th and 75th percentile historical entry RSI readings.
  • Upper Quartile – current daily RSI is above the ticker's normal entry range but still within its usual historical behaviour.
  • Extended – current daily RSI is materially above the ticker's normal entry range and may be stretched even for that name.
  • Limited history – too few historical trades were available to build reliable ticker-specific quartiles.
  • Prudent Target – Investing.com target price reduced by 4% as a prudence haircut before calculating upside.
  • Priority ⭐⭐⭐ – strongest blend of upside to prudent target, favourable entry zone, stronger recent win rate, and deeper trade history.
  • Priority ⭐⭐ – decent setup, but with one or two weaker elements such as lower upside or less favourable entry zone.
  • Priority ⭐ – valid BUY on the system, but lower relative attractiveness versus the other current candidates.
  • Zone Win Rate % – percentage of past trades for this ticker that were profitable when entries occurred within the current Entry Zone. This reflects how effective that zone has historically been for that specific name.
