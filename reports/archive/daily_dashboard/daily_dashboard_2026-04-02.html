BUY section legend

Entry Zone is relative to each ticker's own historical RSI at past strategy entry points, not a fixed universal RSI rule.
  • Lower Quartile – current daily RSI is in the lowest 25% of this ticker's historical entry RSI readings.
  • Normal Range – current daily RSI is between the ticker's 25th and 75th percentile historical entry RSI readings.
  • Upper Quartile – current daily RSI is above the ticker's normal entry range but still within its usual historical behaviour.
  • Extended – current daily RSI is materially above the ticker's normal entry range and may be stretched even for that name.
  • Limited history – too few historical trades were available to build reliable ticker-specific quartiles.
  • Prudent Target – Investing.com target price reduced by 4% as a prudence haircut before calculating upside.
  • Priority / Opportunity Score – current-opportunity ranking for stock BUY candidates, combining cycle timing now, cycle-conditioned win rate, cycle-conditioned median return, and prudent upside still left.
  • Priority ⭐⭐⭐ – top current setup right now: usually fresh or favourable cycle position, good cycle-conditioned return profile, and enough upside left.
  • Priority ⭐⭐ – solid current setup, but with one or two weaker elements such as less upside, weaker cycle-conditioned return, or more mature cycle position.
  • Priority ⭐ – valid system BUY, but currently less attractive than the other names on the board.
  • BUY – immediate execution state only when the system is configured to enter directly without breakout confirmation.
  • ARMED – valid setup detected and breakout level armed; the system is waiting for price to break the stabilisation high before actual entry.
  • BLOCKED – setup is valid, but a blocker is preventing entry.
  • IN_POS – the backtest engine would already be in an active strategy position.
  • WATCH – Golden regime is ON, but no active executable setup is currently present.
  • WAIT – Golden regime is OFF.
  • Zone Win Rate % – percentage of past trades for this ticker that were profitable when entries occurred within the current Entry Zone. This reflects how effective that zone has historically been for that specific name.
  • Cycle Age % – live proxy of how advanced the current Golden cycle is, measured as elapsed weeks since the current Golden regime started divided by the ticker’s median historical Golden cycle length.
  • Cycle Stage – Fresh, Mid, Mature or Late, based on the live cycle-age proxy.
  • Cycle Win Rate 3Y – last 3 years win rate for historical entries made at a similar point in the Golden cycle to today, with small-sample shrinkage toward the full-cycle 3Y win rate.
  • Cycle Median Return 3Y – last 3 years median return for historical entries made at a similar point in the Golden cycle to today, again shrunk toward the full-cycle 3Y median when the bucket is sparse.
  • Cycle Edge – summary marker of whether buying at this stage of the Golden cycle has historically been favourable for this ticker: Early Edge, Mid OK, Late Risk or Exhausted.
